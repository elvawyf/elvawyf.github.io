Modeling Default Probability in SMEs

The financial data variables of SMEs datasets can be coverage to financial ratios, which have been used to analyze and predict firm bankruptcies. The goal is to find related variables and predict the failure risk of each individual in the testing set based on the various models, such as logistic regression and dynamic EB via GLMM discussed in Lai, Su and Sun (2014).
